Our family & yours

A neighborhood center with real heart.

Sunshine Smiles Academy has been part of the North Durham community since 2008. What started as one family’s commitment to warm, dependable childcare has grown into six joyful classrooms — but the heart hasn’t changed. We still know every child by name, celebrate every milestone, and treat your family like our own.

When you walk through our doors, you’ll feel it: kids who are genuinely happy, teachers who genuinely care, and a place that genuinely feels like home.
