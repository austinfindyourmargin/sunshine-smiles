# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Parents, guardians, and families in North Durham who are comparing daycare, childcare, preschool, infant care, toddler care, and pre-kindergarten programs. They are often busy, emotionally invested, and evaluating trust quickly on mobile while looking for safety, warmth, teacher quality, communication, curriculum, hours, location, and a clear next step.

## Product Purpose

Sunshine Smiles Academy's website should convert local families into tour requests, phone calls, enrollment inquiries, and confidence. It exists to make parents feel that their child will be known, loved, protected, taught, and encouraged every day in a warm family-owned early learning center.

## Brand Personality

Warm, bright, reassuring, joyful, nurturing, clean, modern, family-centered, educational, and locally trusted. The voice should feel like a caring director speaking clearly to a parent: personal, steady, specific, and welcoming.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic daycare templates, cluttered cartoon graphics, harsh primary colors, sterile corporate school-district styling, tiny text, overcomplicated dropdowns, stock-photo-heavy pages, hidden phone or address information, and anything that feels cheap, chaotic, or visually overwhelming.

## Design Principles

1. Lead with trust before detail. Parents should see safety, warmth, real people, and the tour CTA immediately.
2. Make the real center visible. Use authentic staff, classroom, playground, building, and program photography as proof, not decoration.
3. Reduce parent anxiety. Keep copy plain, forms simple, contact details obvious, and pages easy to scan on mobile.
4. Balance sunshine with polish. The visual system should be happy and bright without becoming childish or messy.
5. Every section should move a family closer to a visit. Schedule a Tour is the primary conversion path, supported by calls, enrollment, programs, and staff trust.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA. Use strong contrast, semantic HTML, visible focus states, keyboard-friendly navigation, labeled forms, readable type, alt text for meaningful images, reduced-motion support, and mobile touch targets of at least 44px.
