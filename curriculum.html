Curriculum

Playful learning with real developmental purpose.

Sunshine Smiles Academy blends warm classroom care with research-based early learning systems that support social-emotional, physical, language, and cognitive growth.

Mother Goose Time

Mother Goose Time provides concrete materials, ideas, and training that inspire children’s curiosity. Its integrated system supports high-quality, research-based early learning through playful games and projects.

The curriculum weaves 33 research-based skills into activities where children naturally grow across social-emotional, physical, language, and cognitive development.

Visit Mother Goose Time

Creative Curriculum

The Creative Curriculum builds children’s confidence, creativity, and critical thinking skills through hands-on, project-based investigations.

It is nationally known for a forward-thinking, rigorously researched approach trusted by early childhood educators across the country.
