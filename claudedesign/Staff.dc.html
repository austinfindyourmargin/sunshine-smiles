Meet the smiling faces

The smiling faces behind Sunshine Smiles Academy.

Our teachers are the heart of everything we do. Many have been with us for years, and most have a warm story about a child they’ll never forget. They’re patient, playful, trained in early childhood care — and they’ll know your little one by name from day one.

🌻 Caring since 2008 👩‍🏫 Trained early educators ❤️ Low child-to-teacher ratios

FOUNDER · SINCE 2008

Erika Byrd

Owner & Director

Erika opened Sunshine Smiles Academy in 2008 with one belief: every child deserves to feel known, safe, and genuinely loved. Nearly two decades later, that belief still shapes every classroom, every hire, and every warm welcome at the door.

“This isn’t just a daycare to me — it’s an extension of my own family. When parents trust us with their child, we don’t take that lightly for a single day.”
